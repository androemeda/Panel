Senior Product Manager

About the role
We are looking for a Senior Product Manager to own the strategy and delivery of a core product area. You will set direction, align engineering and design, and make decisions grounded in data and customer evidence.

Responsibilities
- Define and own the product strategy and roadmap for your area, tied to clear business outcomes.
- Align engineering, design, and leadership around priorities and resolve competing demands.
- Define success metrics, run experiments, and make data-driven product decisions.
- Drive execution from discovery through launch and measure impact after shipping.
- Communicate strategy and tradeoffs clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Requirements (required)
- 5+ years of product management experience, including ownership of a product area.
- Demonstrated ability to set product strategy and connect it to business results.
- Strong stakeholder management and cross-functional leadership.
- Experience defining metrics and using experimentation to drive decisions.
- A track record of shipping meaningful product outcomes.

Preferred
- Experience in a technical product domain.
- Familiarity with analytics tooling and A/B testing platforms.
